About  Me

I am a third-year Computer Engineering student at Recep Tayyip Erdoğan University, with a strong passion for automation, robotics, and artificial intelligence. My journey includes hands-on experience in developing projects that integrate these fields, particularly focused on autonomous vehicle technologies.

I was honored to participate as a finalist in the Teknofest Robotaxi Autonomous Vehicle Competition, where I deepened my skills in autonomous driving algorithms and real-time data processing. Driven by a commitment to continuous learning, I enjoy expanding my knowledge and skills, especially in mechanical systems and innovative technology. Always eager to embrace new challenges, I am motivated to push boundaries and contribute to advancing the fields of robotics and AI.
